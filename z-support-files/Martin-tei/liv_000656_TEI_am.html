When the stomach is stimulated by food of an exciting
5quality a considerable quantity of blood is attracted
to it & the spleen. The quantity is diminished in
the brain & other organs - purgatives have the same
effect in drawing blood to the bowels - the mind
does not act well after a full meal as both
10blood & nervous energy are sent to the stomach. If
one thinks much about stomach or any other
organ too much nervous influence is transmitted
to it & sometimes too much blood too. You need not
be afraid to bleed if you observe fullness of system in
15connection with unusual pains in either
head or any other part. habitual medication
is not good in any case.
