Zambesi River, Africa.


Private                 20th January 1859


My dear Mr Blanche
                      I ought to have written
5you long ago in acknowledgement

10so occupied that I really could not

the gentleman thinking that we
15could not do without him took

20to send him about his business.
I then became "skipper" myself

10done. I value the revolver very

15I have not yet had an opportunity

20stock enables one to take a steady

10-pared. and thanks to you I am very much

15sight it is to see. A large vessel could
