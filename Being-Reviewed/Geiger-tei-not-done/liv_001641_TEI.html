5

The enclosed mess bill of
Mr Rae amounting to £31.16 for
66 days was handed to me by Mr
Jones Paymaster of H.M.S. Lyra
Had it been a small sum I would
10have paid it and put it to the
incidental expenses but it is
more than I possess of public
money though I have recieved

15£17..17..4 I therefore send
the bill on to you and refer

you will be able to explain the 0002

We go up Rovuma today
All promises fairly - No bar - 17

15mouth which is a mile wide
people speak a dialect very
nearly allied to that of Senna,
the men who knew all our
discoveries long ago ought

20to come down with their knowledge before
